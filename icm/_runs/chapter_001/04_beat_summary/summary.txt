Title: Chapter 001

Overall summary:
Chapter 001 chronicles the transition from the world’s long wait for the Avatar through the conclusion of a devastating war to the dawn of peacetime challenges. Beginning with the discovery of Aang by Katara and Sokka, the narrative rapidly moves through Aang’s triumph over Ozai, Zuko’s inauguration as Fire Lord, and the collective pledge with Earth King Kuei to restore peace. However, lingering tensions over Fire Nation colonies in the Earth Kingdom expose deep, unresolved wounds, prompting the core group—Aang, Zuko, Katara, and Sokka—to accept responsibility for steering the delicate process of reunification. Their new initiative, formally christened as ‘The Harmony Restoration Movement,’ unites them with renewed hope and purpose, while setting the stage for the complex, postwar struggles ahead.

Major events:
- Katara and Sokka discover Aang, the Avatar, encased in ice after a hundred-year absence.
- Aang, aided by friends, defeats Fire Lord Ozai, ending the war.
- Zuko is crowned the new Fire Lord amid celebration.
- Aang, Zuko, and Earth King Kuei pledge to restore peace and balance among nations.
- The ongoing presence of Fire Nation colonies in the Earth Kingdom and the lasting scars of war are acknowledged.
- Group tension arises as the friends discuss the colonial problem, leading Zuko to accept responsibility.
- Aang, with Katara and Sokka (the latter reluctantly), volunteers to help oversee the transition.
- Their mission is named ‘The Harmony Restoration Movement,’ launching the next phase of their journey.
